Personal Injury

We represent victims of car accidents, truck collisions, motorcycle crashes, slip and falls, medical malpractice, and wrongful death. Family Law

Divorce, child custody, alimony, adoption, and domestic violence cases require both sensitivity and strength. We guide you through the emotional and legal complexities, always prioritising your children’s wellbeing and your financial future. Our approach combines mediation where possible with hard‑nosed litigation when necessary.

Services include: divorce mediation, custody agreements, prenuptial contracts, domestic violence protection orders.

Corporate Law

From startup formation to mergers and acquisitions, we provide strategic legal counsel that protects your business. We draft and negotiate contracts, handle intellectual property registration, and represent you in commercial disputes. Our goal is to help you grow while minimising legal risks.

We assist with: entity formation, shareholder agreements, employment contracts, and regulatory compliance.
